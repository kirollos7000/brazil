TRADIONS

Brazil is famous for its colorful traditions. Carnival is the biggest party, with parades, music, and dancing.

    • Carnival

    Carnival in Brazil is a big party with parades, music, and dancing. It happens every year before Lent, especially in Rio de Janeiro.People wear colorful costumes and enjoy the lively atmosphere.

    Capoeira

    Capoeira is a special sport and dance from Brazil. It comes from African traditions and the time when there were slaves in Brazil. People move fast and do flips while music plays. Today, capoeira is important for Brazil's tradion and is used for fun and for fighting.

    Family in Brazil

    Family is very important in Brazil. People have big gatherings with food and stories. Neighbors help each other. On holidays like Christmas Eve, families have special meals together. These traditions make families strong.
