Brazil

Brazil is the largest country in South America, known for its vibrant culture, beautiful landscapes, and diverse wildlife. Famous for the Amazon rainforest, Rio de Janeiro's beaches, and its lively music and dance traditions, Brazil is a country full of energy and life.

    • An old and Interesting history

    The history of Brazil is like an exciting story that started from first people who started to live here. The history changed when year 1500 came people from Portugal till Brazil and started to ruling there. Portuguese traditions and their ways of life mixed with the local cultures, and African slaves that have come later to the country.

    Experience Brazil's lively culture

    For children or young people that are looking for adventure Brazil is the best place to be. From the lively streets of Rio de Janeiro to Bahias relaxing beaches where there is something for everyone to explore and experience. Explore the lush rainforests of the Amazon or just relax on the beautiful beaches of Florianopolis. Every part of Brazil offers tourists different experiences and new adventures.

    Diversity everywhere

    Brazil’s population is diverse as its landscape, in other words countries population is mixed like country itself. Indigenous people, Portuguese settlers and Afro-Brazilian people are all important parts of the country’s culture. This diversity is not only reflected in the peoples faces but also in the traditions, languages and Brazilian cuisine that shapes its identity.

    Brazil’s cultural diversity

    Brazil’s cultural diversity is like a colorful mix of different sounds, sights and tastes. From the joyful sounds and music of samba all the way to the dances of capoeira, each aspect carries a story of strength, creativity and celebration. During your journey through this amazing country, you will discover a variety of traditions, each influenced by different cultures and heritages. Brazil’s cultural richness is not only a reflection of its history but also of its living traditions and culture forcing you to explore and embrace this country more and it’s multifaceted identity.

    How to get there and Where to stay

    Today getting to Brazil is easier than ever, compared to back then today you have major international airports cities where you can stay such as Sao Paulo, Rio de Janeiro, and also “Brasilia welcoming flights” from all over the world. Once you are there, there is plenty of places to stay, from cheap hotels to luxury resorts. Regardless of your budget you can always find a comfortable place to stay in Brazil!
